## About Me
I am an AI Researcher at the <a href="https://gtl.csa.iisc.ac.in/hari/lab/" style="color:#4DA49C; font-weight:bold;">Game Theory Lab</a>, Indian Institute of Science, where I work with <a href="https://gtl.csa.iisc.ac.in/hari/" style="color:#4DA49C; font-weight:bold;">Prof. Narahari</a> on frontier models, explainability, cooperative game theory, and AI for Social Good.

I hold a <strong>Master's degree in Computer Science and Automation from IISc</strong>, with a focus on large language models, function calling, multi-agent systems and deep learning.
 
Before joining the Game Theory Lab, I was part of the founding team of an early-stage AI startup building intelligent systems for financial compliance and accounting.
